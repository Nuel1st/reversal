DATA PROTECTION AND PRIVACY POLICY FOR REVERESALPAYMENTS

1. Scope

At ReversalPayments, we are committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of personal information and activities of our users, agents, partners and other identifiable individuals. This policy is set to inform users regarding our guidelines with the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information as they use our service, through any of the channels, including the ReversalPayments web and the USSD (Unstructured Supplementary Service Data).

2. Policy Details

The personal information that we may collect are used for providing and improving the services we render. We will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy. Details of this policy are listed as follows:

  • Log data
  • Data Integrity
  • Security
i. Log Data

We want to inform you that whenever you visit our service, we collect information that your browser sends to us that is called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your computer’s Internet Protocol ("IP") address, browser version, pages of our service that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, your location while using our service and other statistics.

ii. Data Integrity

ReversalPayments will secure and process Personal Information in a way that is harmonious with the purpose for which it has been collected. We take steps to ensure information collected is accurate, reliable, current and relevant to its intended use.

iii. Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it in order to avoid loss, misuse, unauthorized disclosure taking into due consideration the risks involved in the handling and the nature of the Personal Information. Some of the means we use are:

  • educating and training relevant staff to ensure they are aware of our privacy obligations when handling personal information as well as training around social engineering, phishing, spear phishing, and password risks
  • technological security measures, including fire walls, encryption and anti-virus software
  • real-time monitoring of data leakage controls
  • administrative and technical controls to restrict access to personal information on a ‘need to know’ basis
  • layered and comprehensive cyber security defences
  • physical security measures, such as staff security passes to access our premises
  • external technical assessments, security audits and vendor due diligence
  • endpoint security: Anti-virus, portable storage device lockdown, restricted administrative privileges
  • security incident reporting and management.
  • Although we endeavour to use appropriate security measures in protecting personal information, we cannot guarantee the security of data transmitted to us or by us because the transmission of data over the internet (including by e-mail) is never completely secure.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, we advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately, after they are posted on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
